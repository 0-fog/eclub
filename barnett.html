THE EXHIBITION CENTRE FOR
THE LIFE AND USE OF BOOKS

Artist-led reading room, forum and occasional publishing outlet.

The Exhibition Centre for the Life and Use of Books features an evolving portable library, made up of independent publications and books chosen by an invited curator, alongside a permanent donated reference library.
