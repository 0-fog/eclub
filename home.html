The Exhibition Centre for the Life and Use of Books is an interdisciplinary curatorial unit formed in early 2014.

Co-directors Robert Carter, Lauren Velvick and Daniel Fogarty have backgrounds in visual art, design and journalism and through the Life and Use of Books seek to uncover hidden and esoteric cultural histories, and to facilitate meetings and exchanges between divergent audiences.
